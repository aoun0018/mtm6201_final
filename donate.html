Support Our Mission With Your Donations

Your generosity helps us provide food, medical care, and shelter for animals in need. Every donation, big or small, makes a difference in giving pets a second chance at a loving home. Support Pawsitive Futures today and help us continue our life-saving work!

Ways to Give

There’s more than one way to help animals in need. Choose the type of donation that works best for you:

Donate Funds

Your financial gift helps us provide food, medical care, shelter, and rescue support. Whether it’s a one-time donation or monthly giving, every dollar makes a difference.

Donate Now

In-Kind Donations

Prefer to give supplies? We always need food, blankets, toys, and cleaning products. Check out our wish list to see what our animals need most.

View Wish List

Fund & Sponsor

Host a fundraiser, sponsor an event, or support a specific animal or program. It’s a great way to make a lasting impact — solo or with a team.

Where It Goes

Most of your donation provides medical care, meals, and shelter for animals. A small portion supports outreach and operations to help us rescue more.

Learn More

Thank You for Making a Difference

Your kindness helps us rescue, heal, and rehome animals in need. Because of you, a hungry pup gets fed, a sick kitten gets care, and a scared stray gets a second chance. From all of us — two- and four-legged — thank you for being part of our rescue family.
